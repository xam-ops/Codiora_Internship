// About me

Turning ideas
into interfaces


Hi! I'm Alex Rivera, a frontend developer based in Karachi with a deep love for the intersection of design and engineering. I believe great interfaces are invisible — they just work.

I started coding at 16, fell in love with CSS animations, and never looked back. Today I specialise in React, Tailwind, and performance-first development for startups and agencies worldwide.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me contributing to open-source, writing tech articles, or exploring generative art with p5.js.
